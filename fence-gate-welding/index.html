Fence and Gate Welding FAQ

Do you repair sagging or broken metal gates?

Yes. Abel Welding Services repairs many common gate issues including broken welds, sagging frames, and damaged hinge areas.

Can you weld fence sections and metal posts?

Yes. Metal fence sections, posts, rails, and connecting points can often be repaired or reinforced depending on the condition.

Do you work on commercial gates too?

Yes. Fence and gate welding is available for residential properties, businesses, facilities, and managed commercial sites.

Can you reinforce a gate that keeps failing?

In many cases, yes. Reinforcement can help with repeated stress, misalignment, and weak connection points that cause recurring problems.
