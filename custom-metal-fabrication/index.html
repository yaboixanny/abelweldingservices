Custom Metal Fabrication FAQ

What types of custom fabrication do you handle?

We handle many practical metal fabrication needs including supports, frames, railings, stairs, brackets, and made-to-fit steel or aluminum pieces.

Can you fabricate from an existing design or site dimensions?

Yes. Abel Welding Services can work from plans, reference dimensions, or an on-site review depending on the project.

Yes. We support both residential fabrication work and larger commercial or contractor-driven fabrication projects.
